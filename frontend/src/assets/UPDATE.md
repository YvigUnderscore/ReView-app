## üßä Advanced 3D Review
- **New 3D Viewer**: Transitioned from ThreeDView to Google's `<model-viewer>` for better performance and compatibility.

- **Native 3D Conversion**: Improved support for FBX files with server-side conversion to GLTF/GLB (USD coming soon).

- **3D Assets Configuration**:
